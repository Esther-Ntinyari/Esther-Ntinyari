About Me

Hi, I am Esther Ntinyari, a distinguished fashion model based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a wealth of experience as a fashion model trainer and panelist, I am dedicated to providing personalized coaching tailored to each model's unique strengths and areas for improvement. I emphasize the importance of professionalism, punctuality, and a positive work ethic. I am passionate about empowering future models and helping them achieve their dreams.

Experience

I have worked with top designers, photographers, and brands. I have walked the runway at numerous fashion shows, this includes:

  • Miss Birmingham Kenya Nairobi County 2024
  • Beauty of Africa International Pageant( BAIP)Kenya finalist 2024
  • Runners Up Miss Valentine’s Machakos County 24’
  • Miss Machakos University 2024/2025

I have also served as a judge and mentor on various pageants:

  • Kenyatta University Kitui campus 2024
  • Machakos School Cultural week
  • Mr and Miss Githunguri 2024
  • Kenya Medical Training College (KMTC) Machakos
  • Masinga Technical
  • Chuka University 2025
